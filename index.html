SeeHow Smart Cricket Ball

Want to bowl like your favourite cricketers on TV? The first step to bowling faster, spinning and swinging the ball more, and consistently hitting the seam is to train smarter. SeeHow's smart cricket will help you track your bowling parameters, so you can monitor your performance over time and benchmark yourself against your friends and your heros!

Like a regular cricket ball

SeeHow's smart cricket ball looks, weights, feels and plays like a normal cricket ball. It's just smarter -- it contains a sensor to measure data on your bowling.

No setup required

There is no external sensor or additional setup required. Just bowl normally, and our proprietary algorithms will compute your bowling stats and display it on our mobile app in real-time.
